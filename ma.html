Design Principles

01White-first surfaces

Primary backgrounds are always white or Apple's #f5f5f7. Color is reserved for accent and action only.

02Red as signal

Crimson #c8102e is used exclusively for primary actions, titles, and brand marks. Never decorative.

03Breathing room

Generous whitespace via the 8pt grid. Sections never feel cramped.

04Typography does the work

Hierarchy is established through weight and size, not color or decoration.

05Subtle depth

Shadows are light and natural. Cards feel tangible, not flat or floating.

06Product-forward

Image areas are large and uncluttered on neutral gray. Products are always the hero.

07System fonts

SF Pro / system-ui ensures native, fast rendering across all Apple and non-Apple devices.
